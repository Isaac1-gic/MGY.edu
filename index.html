Malawian Genius Youth (MGY) - Learning Platform

MGY is a progressive web app connecting young minds across Malawi through science, education, and innovation.

Key Features:

📚 Educational Courses — Access quality learning content
💬 MGY Room — Collaborative chat and discussion space for questions and quizzes
👤 User Profiles — Connect with peers, share achievements, and build your network
📲 Offline Access — Installable PWA with service worker support for offline learning
🎓 Community Learning — Engage with expert-led content and discussions

Our Mission: Empower Malawian youth to think big, collaborate, innovate, and shape the future through shared learning and community support.

Perfect for:

Students seeking quality educational content
Young innovators and problem-solvers
Anyone passionate about science and education
Collaborative learners building a network
